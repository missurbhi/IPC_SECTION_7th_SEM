The Story Behind FIR Online

FIR Online was born out of a simple observation: the process of filing an FIR was often intimidating, time-consuming, and lacked transparency. In 2023, a small group of civic-minded technologists decided to solve this problem using their expertise in secure web platforms.

Our goal was to create a system that respected the victim's privacy while ensuring immediate and accountable transmission of reports to law enforcement. We believe that by simplifying the first step, we significantly improve the chance of justice being served quickly.
